### Pro Tips

1. **Be specific about style**: Don't just say "make it premium" — say "use style like blogger X, short sentences, vivid imagery, no idioms"
2. **Samples work better than descriptions**: Include 3-5 well-written examples in the memory pack — AI mimics samples 10x better than descriptions
3. **Be clear about taboos**: What not to write, what words not to use, what topics to avoid — the more detailed the better
4. **Record data feedback**: What went viral and why, what flopped and why — these are your most valuable lessons

## Common Prompt Templates

### Let AI Pick Up Writing

```
I'm giving you an account handover pack. Read it completely, then tell me:
1. What this account is about
2. Who the target audience is
3. What the content style is like
4. What the taboo topics are

Confirm you understand before we start writing.

[Attach IRY memory pack]
```

### Write a Viral Xiaohongshu Post

```
Following our account's style, write a Xiaohongshu post on the topic: [topic]

Requirements:
1. Headline with a hook that makes people want to click
2. Grab attention in the first 3 seconds
3. Content with real value, not just empty slogans
4. End with engagement prompt
5. Add 5-8 relevant hashtags

Reference our previous viral style.

[Attach memory pack + viral samples]
```

### Topic Brainstorm

```
Our account positioning is: [positioning]
Target audience is: [audience]

Help me brainstorm 20 topic directions, categorized as:
1. Educational / how-to (5)
2. Emotional resonance (5)
3. Controversial / discussion (5)
4. Interactive (5)

For each topic, give a headline idea and one-sentence core point.
```
